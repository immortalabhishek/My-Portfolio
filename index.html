01 — CRM Automation

CoachHub Pro

Built a complete automated lead pipeline for a coaching institute use case. A student fills a form on the website, a lead is instantly created in Zoho CRM, synced to HubSpot via Zapier, and an AI-personalized welcome email goes out immediately — followed by an automatic 3-day follow-up. Sales team gets real-time WhatsApp alerts on every new lead. All performance data flows into a live Looker Studio dashboard tracking leads, email open rates, and conversions.

Tools: Zoho CRM · Zapier · HubSpot · OpenAI · WATI · Looker Studio ·
